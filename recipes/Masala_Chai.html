Masala Chai

Serves 10+

An Indian inspired chai

Ingredients

  • 5 black teabags
  • 2 litres oat milk
  • 12 green cardamom pods
  • 5cm ginger sliced
  • 5 small cinnamon sticks crushed
  • small handful of black peppercorns
  • pinch of cloves
  • 5 tbsp sugar

Equipment

  • Slow Cooker
  • Pestle & Mortar
  • Teapot
  • Kettle

Instructions

  1. Fill the kettle with approx a litre of water and boil. Pour the boiling water into the teapot with the 5 teabags and brew for 5 minutes.
  2. Whilst the tea is brewing, turn the slow cooker on to a high heat. Using the pestle & mortar, crush the cardamom pods, black peppercorns and cloves. Crush the cinnamon sticks by hand and place all of these ingredients along with the ginger in the slow cooker. Once the tea has brewed, pour it into the slow cooker too.
  3. Close the lid on the slow cooker and leave the tea to brew again for about 30 mins.
  4. Once the 30 mins are up, add the milk. To save time, you can heat the milk up either on the hob or in the microwave prior to pouring it in.
  5. Add the sugar and stir the mix.
  6. Use a ladle and strainer to pour into cups, serve and enjoy!
